Features

  • Create your own adventures in this game by building your own scene using backgrounds, characters, music and props from your favourite Kazoops episode.

  • Save these scenes in your scrapbook and build on your creations
  • Earn stickers and coins by completing challenges
  • Purchase new adventure packs from your favourite episodes
